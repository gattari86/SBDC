Our 4-Step Framework

The proven process we use to build successful digital presences

1

FOUNDATION – Your Website

Your website is your digital home base. It's the first impression customers get, and it needs to work as hard as you do.

Keep it clear

Who you are, what you do, and how to reach you.

Make it professional but easy to manage

You shouldn't need a developer every time you want to update your hours.

Think mobile-first

More than half of online searches come from phones.

How Poppy helps:

Our Brand Launch Essentials includes a custom one-page professional website designed for your brand and industry. It's mobile-responsive, SEO-friendly, and built for easy content management.

Professional Website Features

  • Mobile-responsive design
  • SEO-friendly structure
  • Easy content management
  • Intuitive navigation

SEO Best Practices

  • Local search optimization
  • Customer-friendly keywords
  • Reviews and credibility building
  • Regular content updates
2

VISIBILITY – SEO

If no one can find you, your website is just an expensive digital flyer. SEO ensures you're discoverable when people are searching for what you offer.

Focus on local search

Claim your Google Business Profile and keep info consistent everywhere.

Use customer-friendly keywords

Think "Houston hair salon" not "specialized beauty solutions."

Build credibility

Reviews, blogs, and regular updates improve ranking.

How Poppy helps:

Our setup includes SEO-friendly design from day one, so your website is optimized for visibility right out of the gate.

3

COMMUNITY – Social Media

Social media isn't about shouting offers into the void, it's about connection. Think of it as your neighborhood coffee shop.

Pick platforms where your people are

Focus your energy where your customers spend their time.

Share content that educates, entertains, or solves a problem

Provide value first, sales will follow.

Build relationships, not just sales

Authentic connections drive long-term success.

How Poppy helps:

With your website and logo in place, you can finally move beyond "just Instagram" and establish a full online presence. Our package is perfect for businesses making that social media to web transition.

Social Media Strategy

  • Platform selection
  • Content planning
  • Community engagement
  • Performance tracking

Building Authority

  • Blog posts and articles
  • Educational content
  • Speaking opportunities
  • Consistent publishing
4

AUTHORITY – Expertise

Authority isn't built overnight. It's earned through consistency and value.

Start small

Answer one customer question in a blog post or video.

Grow visibility

Guest articles, workshops, and public speaking all count.

Stay consistent

Trust builds over time.

How Poppy helps:

We deliver custom logo design with multiple concepts, unlimited revisions, and all file formats. Your brand stays consistent across everything you publish — online and offline.
